OSLO TRONDHEIM

Welcome to YogaVisdom.no

To all of you out there who have found me and this website; welcome.

I'm very glad you're here. It is my hope that you will be able to take something from here that will serve as a guiding light in your life.

    

I came to the yoga philosophy in an indirect way. I began to be interested in hatha yoga (physical yoga) as a way to improve my surfing, which at that time (1969) was my whole life. The benefits were immediately obvious, and I started looking into the subject deeper. By reading, I began to practice simple meditation, ate vegetarian food, and spent more and more time in deeper contemplation of the meaning of life. I had always been a traveler, but now I started on an inner journey. Along the way, my world, both the outer and the inner, became more peaceful and calm.


In 1971, I was so incredibly fortunate (not by coincidence I'm sure) to get in touch with my Spiritual Masters. Under their expertise and guidance, I received and began to follow the ancient science called Bhakti Yoga, which I have been following up until this day. And what a beautiful and fulfilling journey it has been!


So I strongly wish that you will have the same chance that I have had, undeserving as I am.


Best wishes,

Balakhilya das

Lost in the World

We invite you to a different lecture: This is about you!

Our society is progressing faster than ever, always with new technology, gadgets, and expectations to keep up with. We are constantly twittering, snapchatting, facebooking, interacting - available both online and offline - and still 4 out of 10 Norwegians experience loneliness (SSB 2015). It has even come to the point where one could claim that “We are never alone - but we feel more lonely than ever.”

But the problem goes much deeper than social media. Even when you are with your family or friends - the closest people you have in your life - you may still feel an emptiness deep inside of you. We may be afraid to talk about it, but actually we are all longing for purpose, longing for something that actually satisfies our heart’s desire. So what is it we are longing for, and where can it be found?

Hear world-known Vedic teacher and Yoga Wisdom expert Balakhilya das speak on the topic of “Lost in the World”. Come and experience for yourself how the ancient, yet highly relevant science of yoga, helps to solve your personal struggles. This enlightening seminar by Balakhilya das will help you on the path toward a life of joy, peace, and harmony. This may just change your life!

OSLO TRONDHEIM

Karma

When you look at the world, many things may seem unfair. Why do bad things happen to good or innocent people?

Most of us have heard of the law of karma, which is described by familiar sayings like, “what goes around comes around,” and “as you sow, so shall you reap.” When we see or experience suffering, for example the suffering of people close to us, children, or even animals, we may feel incredibly helpless, frustrated or even angry. It can be hard to understand and (much less) accept this reality. So what can be done to finally put an end to this suffering?

In this yoga wisdom talk, Balakhilya das explores the problem of karma & suffering, and sheds light on a profound solution to this very real issue that all of us are faced with. He will share the answers to questions such as "Why am I suffering - experiencing diseases, old age, and death?", "What can I do about suffering?" and "What is the real purpose of my existence?"

Balakhilya das will also share valuable insights on the idea of trying to attract more good karma than bad karma. Actually, even good karma has serious disadvantages. But if good karma is not desirable, how can we live in this world without creating karma at all?
Learn about the law of karma and how it concerns your daily life!
